Packing and Preparing

Information regarding Visas, mobile data, weather, and more.

Visas and Customs

In order to enter Japan, you must complete a customs form. You can do this on arrival or here (Visit Japan Web) ahead of time. You do not need to register for a Visa. You will be given a Tourism Visa on arrival. You do not need to register one ahead of time.

Mobile Data

Your ability to use mobile data will depend entirely on your carrier. My carrier (Google Fi) has international coverage for no additional charge, but I'm not sure of the coverage of any other carriers. We will be using a Discord Server for WiFi messaging.
